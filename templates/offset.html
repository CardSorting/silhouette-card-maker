üí° Offset Tips

  • Positive X: Shifts every other page to the right
  • Negative X: Shifts every other page to the left
  • Positive Y: Shifts every other page down
  • Negative Y: Shifts every other page up
  • Typical values: -10 to +10 pixels work for most printers
  • Fine-tuning: Start with larger values, then adjust by 1-2 pixels
