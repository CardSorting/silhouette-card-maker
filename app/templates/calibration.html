🎯 Calibration PDF Generation

Generate calibration PDFs for all paper sizes to help you find the optimal offset values for your cutting machine.

📋 What are Calibration PDFs?

Calibration PDFs contain registration marks and test patterns that help you:

  • Find optimal offset values: Test different X/Y offsets to achieve perfect alignment
  • Verify cutting accuracy: Ensure your machine cuts exactly where intended
  • Test different paper sizes: Each paper size may require different offset values
  • Save time on real projects: Get your offsets right before cutting actual cards

📁 Generated Files

Calibration PDFs will be generated in the calibration/ directory:

  • a4_calibration.pdf - A4 paper calibration
  • letter_calibration.pdf - Letter paper calibration
  • a3_calibration.pdf - A3 paper calibration
  • tabloid_calibration.pdf - Tabloid paper calibration
  • archb_calibration.pdf - Arch B paper calibration
